Add a second Y-axis

Plot the "Scorpio" series on the second y-axis (the Y2-axis).  The Y-axis and Y2-axis have independent ranges and gridline intervals. This enables you to plot data series that have widely differing scales on the same chart.

Chart xml sourceResulting image
<chart>
    <title>Weather Balloon Tracking</title>

    <series>
        <title>Aquarius</title>
        <datum y="1"/>
        <datum y="3"/>
        <datum y="9"/>
        <datum y="8"/>
        <datum y="7"/>
    </series>

    <series>
        <title>Scorpio</title>
        <datum y2="11"/>
        <datum y2="117"/>
        <datum y2="87"/>
        <datum y2="63"/>
        <datum y2="2"/>
    </series>

    <layout>
        <south>
            <legend/>
        </south>
    </layout>
</chart>
