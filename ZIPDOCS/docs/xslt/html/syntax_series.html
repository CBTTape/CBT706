<series>

Syntax

<series style="one or more CSS2 styling attributes">
  <title>The title of this data series</title>
  <datum y="10"/>
  <datum y="30"/>
  <datum y="20"/>
     .
     .
     .
</series>

Synopsis

The <series> tag describes the data points that make up a single line on the chart.

Under the <series> tag you can insert:

  • a <title> tag describing the series title. The default is "Series n" where "n" is the ordinal number of the series within the <chart> tag.
  • a <datum> tag for each point to be plotted
