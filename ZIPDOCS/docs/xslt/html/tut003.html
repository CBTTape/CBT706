Add another series of data points to be plotted

The Y-axis range has automatically been changed again (from 1-9 to 0-130 to cover the new range of Y- values). In this example, the second series has dwarfed the first. Later we will plot one series on the secondary Y-axis to fix the scale mismatch.

Chart xml sourceResulting image
<chart>
    <title>Weather Balloon Tracking</title>

    <series>
        <datum y="1"/>
        <datum y="3"/>
        <datum y="9"/>
        <datum y="8"/>
        <datum y="7"/>
    </series>
    
    <series>
        <datum y="11"/>
        <datum y="117"/>
        <datum y="87"/>
        <datum y="63"/>
        <datum y="2"/>
    </series>
    
</chart>
